What Happens in a Consultation?

Whether you're looking for an engagement ring, thinking about a special gift, or want to redesign an existing piece - the first step is a conversation. We'll sit down (physically, on Zoom, or WhatsApp), understand what matters to you, and show you exactly what's possible - and at what price.

  • We hear what you're looking for (style, budget, occasion)
  • We show relevant options (photos, examples, CAD)
  • We explain natural vs. lab-grown differences
  • We give a transparent quote - no surprises
  • You decide on your own time. No pressure. Period.
