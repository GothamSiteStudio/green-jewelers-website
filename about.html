The Story Behind the Sparkle

A decade of wholesale diamond experience. Built on trust, transparency, and craftsmanship.

Green Jewelers didn't start in a showroom. It started behind the scenes - in the wholesale diamond district, where the real deals happen before a stone ever reaches a display case. For over a decade, we've been sourcing, grading, and trading diamonds at the wholesale level. We know the market from the inside out.

After years of working with retailers, we saw the same thing over and over: huge markups, impersonal service, and customers who didn't know what they were really paying for. We decided to change that. Green Jewelers was born to give individuals and families the same access, the same pricing, and the same quality that was previously reserved for the trade.

We don't operate like a jewelry store. There's no glass counter, no salesperson on commission, no upselling. Just you, us, and a conversation about what you want. Every piece we create starts with listening. What's the style? What's the budget? What's the story behind this piece? That's where we begin.

We're proud to serve the Jewish community across the Tri-State area. From engagement rings for young couples in Lakewood and Brooklyn, to anniversary gifts in the Five Towns, to custom Chai pendants and Judaica pieces - we understand the occasions, the traditions, and the expectations. This isn't just business. It's personal.

Every diamond comes with a GIA or IGI certificate. Every price is transparent. Every client gets the same dedicated attention whether it's a $1,500 ring or a $50,000 piece. And if something isn't perfect? We'll make it right. That's not a policy - that's how we work.
